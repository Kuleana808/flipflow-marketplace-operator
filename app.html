Actual usable MVP

FlipFlow helps marketplace sellers rewrite listings, handle buyers faster, and track inventory without DM chaos.

This is the first real product surface, not just an opportunity page. Paste a raw Facebook Marketplace-style listing, generate better versions for other networks, create reply scripts for flaky buyers, and track inventory plus no-show risk in one place.

What this MVP already does

  • Transforms one rough listing into cleaner versions for Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, and OfferUp-style formats.
  • Generates buyer replies for “Is this available?”, lowball offers, pickup coordination, and ghosting recovery.
  • Stores active inventory in-browser with price, network, status, and no-show risk tags.
  • Lets us test real seller workflows before building logins, messaging integrations, or automation rails.
